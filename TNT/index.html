Top fun activities to do while in Uganda

Gorilla Trekking

Gorilla trekking in Uganda is the premier wildlife experience, taking you deep into the jungles of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park or Mgahinga Gorilla National Park.

Mountain Climbing

Uganda offers world-class mountain climbing, ranging from multi-day glacial treks in the Rwenzoris to accessible volcano hikes in the Virunga ranges.
